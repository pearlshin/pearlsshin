About Me


I am passionate about turning ideas into a reality. The process of finding unique solutions to complex problems to create something that is functional, creative and enjoyable is something that I find worthwhile. I come from a liberal arts education and editorial background, but the lack of ability to build my ideas and express my interests sparked my interest in programming. Pursuing a career in technology has led me to find software engineering as a satisfying medium to build and express an idea. - Pearl Shin
